Our Story

Built for Longevity

Two decades of craftsmanship, trust, and family values — shaping homes across Maharashtra.

Our Heritage

A Foundation Built on Honesty

Shree Gajanan Construction was founded by Rakesh Shelke with a single, clear purpose: to build homes that families could rely on for generations. Starting with a civil engineering diploma and an unshakeable commitment to quality, Rakesh grew the company from a small local contractor into one of the most trusted construction names in the Nashik region.

Over 21 years, we have completed more than 110 projects — each one a reflection of our belief that a home is not just a structure, but a legacy. In 2026, that dedication was recognized with the Star Performer Award, a testament to the trust our clients have placed in us year after year.

Today, Shree Gajanan Construction continues to be a family-run business at heart — personal, accountable, and deeply invested in the success of every project we undertake.

Recognition

Star Performer Award 2026

Rakesh Shelke, founder of Shree Gajanan Construction, was honoured with the Star Performer Award in 2026 — a recognition of his outstanding contribution to the construction industry and his unwavering commitment to quality, integrity, and client satisfaction over more than two decades of service.

The People Behind the Work

Meet the Team

Rakesh Shelke
Founder & Owner

A civil engineering diploma holder with 21 years in the field, Rakesh founded Shree Gajanan Construction with a simple belief: that every home should be built to last generations. His hands-on approach to every project remains the foundation of the company today.

Sagar Shingade
Site Supervisor

Sagar oversees the day-to-day rhythm of every site, making sure each project stays on schedule, on budget, and built to the standard our clients expect. His eye for detail keeps every job running smoothly from groundwork to handover.

Pitamber
Slab Construction & Centering Specialist

Pitamber leads our slab construction and centering work, where precision matters most. His careful, methodical approach ensures every structural foundation is built strong, true, and ready to support the home above it.
