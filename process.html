How We Work

The Construction Journey

From the first conversation to the final handover — a clear, transparent process designed around you.

01 / Consultation

We Begin With a Conversation

Every project starts with listening. We sit down with you — at your convenience — to understand your vision, your requirements, your budget, and your timeline. There are no standard packages here; every home we build starts from scratch, shaped entirely around your needs. This first meeting is free, informal, and completely without obligation.

02 / Planning & Design

Turning Vision Into Blueprint

Once we understand your goals, we move into detailed planning. This includes site assessment, structural planning, material selection, and cost estimation. We walk you through every decision — nothing happens without your approval. You will know exactly what is being built, how it will look, and what it will cost before a single brick is laid.

03 / Construction

Built With Precision, Every Day

This is where our team's expertise truly shows. Under the direct supervision of Rakesh Shelke and our site supervisor Sagar Shingade, construction proceeds methodically — from foundation and slab work to brickwork, plastering, and finishing. You are kept informed at every stage, with regular updates and full transparency on progress and any decisions that arise on site.

04 / Handover

Your Home, Ready to Live In

The final stage is one we take great pride in. Before we hand over your home, we conduct a thorough quality check — walking through every room, every finish, every fitting — to make sure everything meets our standard and yours. Only when you are completely satisfied do we consider the project complete. And even after handover, we remain available for any questions or concerns.

Our Commitment

No Surprises. No Shortcuts.

In 21 years of construction, we have learned that the best projects are built on clear communication and mutual trust. We never cut corners, never hide costs, and never leave a client in the dark. That is the Shree Gajanan promise — and it is why more than 110 families have trusted us with their most important investment.
